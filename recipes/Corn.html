Corn on the Cob

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 ear fresh corn, in the husk
  • 1 teaspoon butter, or more to taste
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Microwave ear of corn on high until cooked through and tender, about 3 1/2 minutes.
  2. Transfer ear to a cutting board. Hold the tapered end with a towel and cut 1 inch off the bottom of the thick end. Squeeze the tapered end until the ear comes out of the husk and silk.
  3. Spread butter over corn and season with salt.
