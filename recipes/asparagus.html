Asparagus

Description

I have always been good at sauteeing asparagus, but this is one of my favorite methods to create a sweet and traditional appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter, or more as needed
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium-low heat. Cook asparagus in melted butter, stirring a few times, until tender, 6 to 8 minutes.
  2. Drizzle honey over the asparagus and stir to coat; season with garlic powder and cayenne pepper.
